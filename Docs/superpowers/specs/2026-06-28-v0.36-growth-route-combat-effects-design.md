# v0.36 Growth Route Combat Effects Design

## Goal

Make the active in-run growth route affect combat directly. v0.35 made routes readable and reward-biased; v0.36 gives each route a small combat effect so the player can feel the route during the same run.

## Scope

- Add route effect query methods to `RunManager`.
- Apply Player route to player attack damage.
- Apply Counterplay route to dodge counter damage duration/multiplier and Guard opening damage.
- Apply AI route to AI companion attack cooldown.
- Apply Survival route to bond rescue retained HP.
- Apply Build route as a temporary extra AI Build level for tactical support tuning.
- Show route effects in existing status labels where practical.

## Out of Scope

- No new active skills.
- No route-specific VFX pass.
- No permanent route unlocks or save schema changes.
- No reward rarity system.
- No full balance pass.

## Route Effects

- Player route: player outgoing attack damage gains a small multiplier per route level.
- Counterplay route: successful dodges create a brief attack boost even without the dodge reward, and Guard opening hits gain a route multiplier.
- AI route: companion basic attack cooldown is multiplied down while the route is active.
- Survival route: bond rescue holds the player at extra HP.
- Build route: current AI Build tuning is evaluated as if it had an extra temporary upgrade level.

## Architecture

`RunManager` remains the source of route state. It exposes combat-effect properties such as `PlayerRouteDamageMultiplier`, `CounterplayRouteDodgeBoostDurationBonus`, `CompanionRouteCooldownMultiplier`, `SurvivalRouteRescueHealthBonus`, and `BuildRouteBonusLevel`.

Combat components read those properties at the moment they resolve their behavior. This keeps reward route logic centralized and avoids permanently mutating component base values when the route changes.

## Testing

EditMode tests cover:

- Player route increases effective player damage.
- Counterplay route enables dodge counter damage without requiring the dodge reward.
- AI route lowers companion effective cooldown.
- Survival route increases effective bond rescue HP.
- Build route grants a temporary tactical support upgrade level.
